The Executive Board

The executive board of the B.O.N.D. consists of three members who work diligently to make sure the organization is holding the values it was created on and ensuring that men have a safe place to commune and connect on the campus of the University of Miami.

The executive board positions include: president, vice president, freshman liason and public relations chair.
