Our Events

Throughout the course of the school year, the executive board of Brothers Overcoming Negativity and Destruction hosts several events to spark community engagement and geared towards issues we face in our daily lives.

These events include forums on peer to peer relationships, sexual eduacation, resume building workshops, money management, and more. These events are an excellent opportunity to meet new friends and engage in interesting conversation. You never know what you may learn.
