What is B.O.N.D. ?

Brothers Overcoming Negativity and Destruction, or B.O.N.D., and we are an organization based on the following principles:

  • To promote male pride by self-enrichment and self-education.
  • To provide general academic, social and leadership support for minority males by incorporating all aspects of student life.
  • To establish leadership to provide political and social change on the University of Miami campus.
  • To promote academic achievement in the classroom as well as increase knowledge of self outside the classroom.
  • To remove all negative stereotypes associated with minority males by maintaining a positive image To serve as exemplary models for minority males on campus and in the broader community.
